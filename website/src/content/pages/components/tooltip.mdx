## Anatomy

To set up the tooltip correctly, you'll need to understand its anatomy and how we name its parts.

> Each part includes a `data-part` attribute to help identify them in the DOM.

<Anatomy id="tooltip" />

```tsx
<Tooltip.Root>
  <Tooltip.Trigger />
  <Tooltip.Positioner>
    <Tooltip.Arrow>
      <Tooltip.ArrowTip />
    </Tooltip.Arrow>
    <Tooltip.Content />
  </Tooltip.Positioner>
</Tooltip.Root>
```
